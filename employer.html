HOW IT WORKS

Hire the cream of the cream.

APPLI's predictive analytics engine matches top sales talent with high-growth companies based on skill-set, personality traits, and compensation metrics.

A Strategic Partnerships Manager creates your Company Match Profile based on an in-depth analysis of your company culture, goals, values, and hiring needs.

Our algorithm determines which candidates meet the affinity threshold to match with your company. Eligible candidates are automatically notified of the match and given the option to accept or reject the opportunity..

It is therefore up to you, to set interview dates with the potential candidates.

As soon as your account is created, you will be able to post jobs and will recieve a list of shortlisted candidates based on your degree, exprience and the personal traits you are looking for in an employee.
